Eggy In The Middle Bread Recipe

A delicious runny yolk egg cooked in the center of a piece of toast and eaten with a fork is a great way to enjoy an egg in a slightly different way. My kids feel fancy when eating these.

Ingredients

  • Two teaspoons bacon grease
  • 1 slice of bread
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and ground black pepper

Recipe and Cooking Information

  • Prep: 1 mins
  • Cook: 4 mins
  • Servings: 1

Step 1

Melt the bacon grease in a non stick fry pan over low heat.

Step 2

Cut a 1 1/2 to 2-inch hole from the center of the bread slice; lay in the hot skillet. When the side facing down is lightly toasted, about 2 minutes, flip and crack the egg into the hole; season with salt and pepper. Continue to cook until the egg is cooked and mostly firm. Flip again and cook 1 minute more to assure doneness on both sides. Serve immediately. Enjoy!
