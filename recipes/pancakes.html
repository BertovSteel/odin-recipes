Easy Pancake Recipe

It's the simple pancake recipe that's gone viral worldwide - you'd 'batter' give it a go!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups self-raising flour
  • 1/4 tsp Coles Bicarbonate Soda
  • 1/3 cup caster sugar
  • 25g butter, melted
  • Butter, to serve
  • Maple Syrup

Recipe and Cooking Information

  • Prep: 5 mins
  • Cook: 10 mins
  • Servings: 10

Step 1

Whisk milk Unsure of the quantity needed? Click on the underlined ingredient to reveal the quantity. No need to flip back and forth!, egg and vanilla together in a jug. Sift flour and bicarbonate of soda into a bowl. Stir in sugar. Make a well in centre. Add milk mixture. Whisk until just combined.

Step 2

Heat a large non-stick frying pan over medium heat. Brush pan with butter. Using 1/4 cup mixture per pancake, cook 2 pancakes for 3 to 4 minutes or until bubbles appear on surface. Turn and cook for 3 minutes or until cooked through. Transfer to a plate. Cover loosely with foil to keep warm. Repeat with remaining mixture, brushing pan with butter between batches. Serve. Enjoy!
